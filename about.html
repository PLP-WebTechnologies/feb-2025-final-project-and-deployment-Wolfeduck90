Our Mission

Threads of Humanity was created to bring light to the untold stories of addiction recovery. We believe that healing goes beyond substances—addiction comes in many forms, and recovery is a journey worth sharing.

The Story Behind This Blog

Founded by an individual who once felt lost in the grips of addiction, this blog is a safe space for voices yearning to be heard. The power of shared experiences fosters hope, resilience, and connection.
